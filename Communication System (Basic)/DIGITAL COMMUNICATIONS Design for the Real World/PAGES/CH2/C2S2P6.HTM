Information transfer rate

 

The information transfer rate for a data channel is defined as the speed at which binary information (bits) can be transferred from source to destination.

Units of Information Transfer Rate --> bits/second

 

For example, if six bits of information are sent every six ms, then,

Information transfer rate = 6 bits / 6 ms = 1000 bits/second
